Following my passion
Background story
Design has always been my passion and hobby since my early childhood. Although I studied engineering the traditional way at universities, I learned to design on my own out of curiosity.
This curiosity helped me learning every single day to ensure I can deliver the most modern and effective solutions. I believe in working hard and smart instead of searching for “hacks and shortcuts”.
Making people happy by making things simple
Why design?
As technology progresses things are getting more and more complex. As a designer, I have a special opportunity to make these complex processes simple, delightful and joy to
use every day.
Design allows us to differentiate ourselves by showing attention and care about our customers and users. Everybody values design either consciously or unconsciously. This is why I love creating thoughtful, meaningful and polished experiences, which makes both the creators and customers happy.
In case you would like to know more about me, my work or have any questions, feel free to send me an email anytime.
